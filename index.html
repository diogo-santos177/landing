About Us

Recife Secreto is one of the most diverse frozen seafood distributors in the Algarve, Portugal. With a portfolio of over 100 species, the brand offers products in the categories of fish, seafood, cephalopods and cod.

Besides having a wide selection of seafood products, Recife Secreto differentiates itself in the market by its specialization in Monkfish and Shrimp, the Star Products!
